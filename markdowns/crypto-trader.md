# Crypto Trader

## Summary
Build a crypto trading agent that can analyze opportunities and automate parts of the trading workflow over time, likely in phases.

## Problem
Crypto markets move quickly, data is fragmented, and manual monitoring/trading can be time-consuming and emotionally inconsistent.

## Proposed Automation
Create a crypto trading agent that helps monitor markets, identify setups or signals, and support or automate parts of the trading process. The system may include data gathering, signal generation, risk filters, execution support, and performance tracking.

The workflow will likely need to be built in phases rather than all at once.

Possible phase progression:
- Phase 1: monitoring, summaries, and alerts
- Phase 2: semi-automated trade preparation and approval workflows
- Phase 3: more automated execution with risk controls

## Potential Users
- active crypto traders
- retail swing traders
- systematic traders
- crypto investors seeking automation

## Monetization Ideas
- internal trading tool
- premium signal product
- trading assistant subscription
- managed strategy research workflow

## Questions / Unknowns
- What should Phase 1 include exactly?
- What trading style is intended: scalping, day trading, swing trading, or longer-term positioning?
- Which markets matter most: BTC, ETH, majors, altcoins, perpetuals, or spot only?
- What risk controls would be required before any execution layer?
- Should execution eventually happen through exchange APIs, alerts only, or approval-first automation?
