## Highest-Priority Verified Shortlist

### 1) MSU Denver Scholarships
Description: Best first stop because the student already attends MSU Denver, and the institutional scholarship process can surface multiple awards through one pathway. This should likely be the first operational search lane.
Link: <https://www.msudenver.edu/scholarships/>

### 2) MSU Denver AcademicWorks Scholarship Portal
Description: Direct scholarship application portal for MSU Denver opportunities. High practical value because it is closer to live opportunity workflow than generic school information pages.
Link: <https://msudenver.academicworks.com/?utf8=%E2%9C%93&page=3>

### 3) MSU Denver Latino Faculty Staff Association Scholarship
Description: Strong school-linked Hispanic/Latino scholarship lead. Particularly relevant because it combines institutional fit with Latino community relevance.
Link: <https://www.msudenver.edu/latino-faculty-staff-association/scholarship/>

### 4) Latin American Educational Foundation (LAEF)
Description: One of the strongest Colorado-specific Hispanic scholarship leads. Good fit for a Colorado student and likely one of the most important non-institutional sources for this profile.
Link: <https://www.laefcolorado.org/programs>

### 5) Hispanic Scholarship Fund (HSF)
Description: Strong national Hispanic scholarship source. Competition may be broader, but it remains a core scholarship source for this student profile.
Link: <https://www.hsf.net/scholarship>

### 6) MSU Denver Financial Aid and Scholarships
Description: Useful supporting source for scholarship timelines, financial-aid interplay, and institutional process details.
Link: <https://www.msudenver.edu/financial-aid/scholarships/>

### 7) Denver Scholarship Foundation Directory PDFs
Description: Good Denver/Colorado regional scholarship discovery source. Useful for finding local scholarships that may have lower competition than national awards.
Link: <https://denverscholarship.org/students-alumni/scholarship-directory/download-pdfs/>

### 8) Denver Scholarship Foundation Institutional Scholarships
Description: Useful for institutional scholarship discovery in the Denver ecosystem and worth checking for school-linked opportunities.
Link: <https://denverscholarship.org/students-alumni/scholarship-directory/download-pdfs/dsf-scholarship-directory-institutional-scholarships/>

### 9) Jeffco Schools Foundation Scholarships
Description: Strong local Colorado source worth reviewing for regional/community opportunities.
Link: <https://www.jeffcoschoolsfoundation.org/scholarships.html>

### 10) CCCS Foundation Scholarships
Description: Useful Colorado system-level source, especially if community-college-connected or transfer-related scholarships are relevant.
Link: <https://www.cccs.edu/cccs-foundation/scholarships/>
