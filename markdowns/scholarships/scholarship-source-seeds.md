## Notes
- Prefer official, institutional, and trusted foundation sources first.
- Treat commercial aggregators as discovery aids, not authoritative truth.
- Re-check deadlines and eligibility on the sponsor page before acting.
- Naviance links may require student/school access and may not be broadly fetchable.
- FAFSA and state education pages are useful context sources, but not usually direct scholarship listings.
