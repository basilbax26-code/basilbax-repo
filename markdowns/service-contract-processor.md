# Service Contract Processor

## Summary
Build an agent that helps process legal contracts for service businesses such as HVAC, construction, and similar trades.

## Problem
Service businesses often deal with contracts, estimates, scopes, terms, and supporting paperwork that take time to review, organize, and process manually. These documents can be inconsistent, repetitive, and difficult to manage at scale.

## Proposed Automation
Create an agent that helps process legal and operational contract documents for service businesses. The workflow could include intake, document classification, extraction of key terms, summarization, risk flagging, comparison against expected clauses, and preparation of structured outputs for review.

## Potential Users
- HVAC companies
- construction companies
- home service businesses
- subcontractors
- office/admin teams handling contracts

## Monetization Ideas
- contract review support subscription
- per-document processing fee
- contractor admin workflow product
- white-label service-business back-office tool

## Questions / Unknowns
- What does “process” mean in practice: classify, summarize, extract terms, compare, risk-flag, draft, or route?
- What document types matter most: customer agreements, subcontractor agreements, bids, change orders, NDAs, or insurance/compliance documents?
- Is the goal internal ops support, legal review support, or customer-facing document generation?
- What level of accuracy and human review is required?
- Which outputs would be most useful: summaries, clause checklists, extracted fields, red flags, or workflow routing?
