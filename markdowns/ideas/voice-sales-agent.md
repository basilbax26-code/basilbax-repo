# Voice Sales Agent

## Summary
AI voice assistant that answers inbound calls for small businesses, qualifies leads, and routes the best ones to a human.

## Problem
Small businesses miss inbound leads after hours, during busy periods, or when staff cannot answer immediately.

## Proposed Automation
Use an AI voice workflow to answer calls, ask qualification questions, collect contact information, summarize intent, and either book or route follow-up.

## Potential Users
- home service businesses
- dental offices
- med spas
- local agencies

## Monetization Ideas
- monthly SaaS fee
- usage-based minutes
- premium booking integrations

## Questions / Unknowns
- Which vertical has the highest pain and willingness to pay?
- Is voice quality good enough for frontline sales intake?
- What integrations matter most for early users?
