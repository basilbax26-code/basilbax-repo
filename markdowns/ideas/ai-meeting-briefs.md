# AI Meeting Briefs

## Summary
Turn transcripts and raw meeting notes into concise operator-grade briefs with decisions, actions, owners, and risks.

## Problem
Meetings generate too much unstructured text and too little follow-through.

## Proposed Automation
Ingest transcript or notes, extract decisions and action items, summarize key risks, and produce a short structured brief.

## Potential Users
- founders
- chiefs of staff
- project managers
- leadership teams

## Monetization Ideas
- per-seat SaaS pricing
- team plans
- premium integrations with meeting tools

## Questions / Unknowns
- Which meeting sources should be supported first?
- How much editing do users expect before sharing?
- Is the main value summary quality or action extraction?
