# Lead Follow-Up Bot

## Summary
Automate immediate follow-up for inbound leads using SMS and email so fewer leads go cold.

## Problem
Many businesses respond too slowly to inbound leads, which reduces conversion.

## Proposed Automation
Trigger fast personalized follow-up, classify replies, and escalate warm leads to a human or booking workflow.

## Potential Users
- local service businesses
- consultants
- agencies
- clinics

## Monetization Ideas
- monthly subscription
- per-lead pricing
- managed service upsell
